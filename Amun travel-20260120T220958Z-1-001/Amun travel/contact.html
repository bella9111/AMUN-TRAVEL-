Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

You can modify or cancel your booking through your account dashboard. For bookings made within the last 24 hours or for complex changes, please contact our support team. Please note that cancellation policies vary depending on the service provider.

  • Hotels: Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in
  • Tours: Free cancellation up to 7 days before the tour date
  • Transportation: Changes may incur fees based on provider policies

Our AI itinerary generator uses machine learning to create personalized travel plans based on:

  1. Your travel preferences (from our questionnaire)
  2. Budget constraints
  3. Duration of stay
  4. Seasonal factors and local events
  5. User reviews and ratings

The system optimizes for travel time between locations and balances popular attractions with hidden gems.
